Mars is a rich destination for scientific discovery and robotic and human exploration like no other. Its formation and evolution are comparable to Earth, helping us learn more about our own planet's history and future. Mars had conditions suitable for life in its past that we know it true. Future exploration could uncover evidence of life even answering one of the fundamental mysteries of the cosmos. "Are we alone?" With your help we could alter the course of history and bring about a new golden age for humanity.
